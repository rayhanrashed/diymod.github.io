Setting Up Your Filters

Chat Interface

Create filters using natural language. Simply describe what content you want to filter in conversational terms.

Filter Configuration

Customize intensity and duration settings. Set filters to be temporary or permanent based on your needs.

Getting Started

Click the DIY-MOD extension icon in your browser toolbar to access the chat interface and options panel.

What to Expect

Processing Delay

Content filtering involves AI processing, which may take a few moments. You'll see loading indicators during this time.

Works on Text & Images

The extension processes both textual content and images on supported platforms (Reddit, X/Twitter).

Account Optional

You can use DIY-MOD without logging in. Sign in with Google to sync settings across devices.

Performance Note

Initial processing may be slower as the system learns your preferences. Performance improves with use.

How Interventions Look

Content Warnings

Sensitive content is covered with customizable warning overlays. Click to reveal if desired.

Content Modification

Text and images can be altered or replaced based on your filter settings. Modified content is clearly marked.

Visual Markers

All modified content includes visible markers (like "MOD" tags) so you know when content has been processed by your filters.
